Spooky information about us

The Ghost Hotel was built in 1873 by the Smith's family and has been passed down for several generations until it closed down in 1987 due to paranormal activity. Stories say that the Smith's family lost their 5 year old son when he fell down a flight of stairs and hit his head on the stone floor when the hotel first was built. Legends say that the son's spirit still lives at the hotel and roams the corridors at night to play and guests have heard whispers of a child's voice.

We are five new owners of the famous Ghost Hotel to keep the legend alive and the business going. We hope that the guests that come to visit enjoy their stay with a little curiosty from the activity of the underworld.
